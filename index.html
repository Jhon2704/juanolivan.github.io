Selected Case Study

Industrial impact snapshot

Industrial label inspection optimization for regulated production

Improved an industrial visual inspection workflow by redesigning the inspection strategy around non-critical masking and retraining. The goal was not only better model accuracy, but a lower reject rate under real production constraints and a validation-friendly deployment approach.

0.78% → 0.09% Reject rate reduction after masking strategy and retraining in production-oriented evaluation.
×8.6 lower Relative reject-rate improvement while preserving focus on true defects.
Validated mindset Approach designed for regulated environments where traceability and deployment discipline matter.
Problem

The initial inspection setup generated unnecessary rejects because non-critical zones were influencing the decision boundary in a real manufacturing scenario.

Solution

Applied masking on non-critical regions, retrained the model and re-evaluated the system with production-oriented evidence, including ROC and confusion-matrix based reasoning.

Why it matters

This is the kind of work recruiters care about: not just building a model, but adapting it to industrial reality, reducing operational friction and making it deployable.
